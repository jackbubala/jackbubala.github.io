The State of the Chicago Bears

Hello everyone and welcome to the state of the Chicago Bears. Throughout this blog I will be going over the difficult decision that lies in front of Ryan Pace, picking the right quarterback to lead the Bears into the future. It seems as though he’s already made his decision, having recently signed Andy Dalton. In this blog post I will be going over whether Pace made the right choice as well as what college quarterback should be drafted in order to be Dalton’s mentee that will someday be able to bring the Bears back to the Super Bowl.
